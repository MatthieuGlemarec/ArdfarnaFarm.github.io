Join Us

Roll up your sleeves and join us in one of our hands on courses in sustainable living and celebrate the beauty and importance of Irish nature. Courses are open to everyone and are run monthly either on the Ardfarna property or the near by surrounds. We can't make a difference all on our own. We are all about learning, sharing and growing as a community and for this reason each course is offered on a "Pay what you can & pay it forward" ideology.
