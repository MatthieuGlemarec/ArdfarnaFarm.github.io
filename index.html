Welcome to Ardfarna Organic Farm

Rooted in Nature, Grown in Ardfarna

Ardfarna Farm sits on a humble 10 acres property just outside of the coastal town of Bundoran Co. Donegal. Our mission is to nurture and learn from the land that gives us life with the utmost respect. We want to share that knowledge and passion with as many people as possible, and in doing so bring balance, nature and health back into our society. We grow small-batch produce utilising permiculture principles, run monthly courses on sustainable living, host nature and foreaging walks, herbal medicine workshops and celebrate the beauty and importance of Irish nature.
