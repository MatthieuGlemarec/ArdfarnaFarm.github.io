Our Story

How a Dream Became a Reality

Ardfarna Organic Farm began with a shared vision: to reconnect with the land and embrace a simpler, more sustainable way of living. What started as an overgrown plot outside Bundoran has blossomed into a thriving farm, thanks to dedication, hard work, and a lot of love. Today, Ardfarna Farm is a hub for education, connection, and growth.

Meet Mia and Matthieu

The Heart and Soul of Ardfarna Farm

Mia and Matthieu are passionate about sustainable living and community building. With Mia's background in permaculture design and herbal medicine and Matthieu's muscles and money, the pair complement each other perfectly. Their shared love for natural Ireland and commitment to community stewardship of the land inspired the creation of Ardfarna Farm.

Why We Do It

Our Mission and Vision

At Ardfarna Farm, we believe in the power of nature to heal and inspire. Our mission is to provide a space where people can reconnect with the earth, learn sustainable practices, and embrace a healthier way of life. Whether it's through our courses, our produce, or simply spending time on the farm with us, we aim to make sustainability accessible and meaningful for all.
